Current position

I currently work in Nice, France, as research scientist for Neurodec.

My colleagues and I are developing a software capable of simulating highly realistic, personalized datasets of electromyography (EMG) signals of arbitrary size and variability for training industrial AI-based algorithms.

Research interests

At the moment, my scientific focus is on

  • efficient GPU implementations of Finite Elements engines;
  • design of integrator-solutor pairs in the context of Exponential Integration of stiff PDEs.

However, my research interests include a host of topics from the wider field of High Performance Scientific Computing, such as

  • Numerical Linear Algebra, mostly in the Functions of Matrices field;
  • Arbitrary Precision arithmetic scientific computing;
  • Interpolation Theory;
  • numerical methods for Optimal Control problems.

You can find a list of my publications on my Google Scholar page.
